MYKOLA

Mykola is a 3d narrative-driven deck-building adventure game. Traverse to the afterlife and play a mysterious card game with the sad dead cowboys to save your brother's soul.

Helena is a fortune teller now and a gang member in the past. She gave up on criminal life when her brother died many years ago.Everything changes when her brother returns from the dead and announces he is in trouble.
