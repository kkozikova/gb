IREVERSE

You find yourself a passerby in a virtual world that slowly dissipates as its being forgotten. Driven by nostalgia and your nature of passing by, you pick your way through the town, spreading flowers and plants in your steps.

A video game/ interactive installation created as a part of my first masters thesis. It is centered around exploring a digital/ artificial world through the usage of made up flower species.

Reception

The feature that brought the most attention was the contrast between the colorful internet aesthetics and the theme of inevitably being forgotten.
It is another experience-based titles, originally tied to its place/station in a gallery and is currently being remade into a standalone game.
