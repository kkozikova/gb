Aris Arcanum

Lovecraftian roguelite set in an ancient library. The player takes the role of a fired library employee who is trying to gather the sacred knowledge of the library for themselves. Unlike in Hades this character comes with a disadvantage the player will need to learn to manage in order to complete their goal.
The vast majority of the game was developed in 4 months by a team of student game devs called Broken Cane.

Find more on the Official Page!

Reception

The title was recieved well for artstyle as well as its unique take on the standard roguelite gameplay, which puts the title more into a niche category rather than a mainstream one.
