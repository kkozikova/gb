THUS SANG THE TREES

UI/UX || Tutorialisation ||Level Design

A mix of gardening sim and horror highlighting the importance of cooperation with nature. Project of multiple masters students where I acted as a vision holder and team leader.

itch.io

The unusual gameplay promoting cooperative themes with a non-human agent required a lot of iteration in tutorialisation. The end product combines subtle cues in the level design with pop ups to optimize the learning curve and achieve the designed experience.

IREVERSE

|| Solo Endeavour ||

Situated in a world of vaporvawe and internet aesthetics a passerby finds their way around with the help of plants.

The project was placed in a gallery space. The controls were to be as simple as possible, just like the learning curve. The internet aesthics allowed much freedom in designing the UI.

Goating Over It

UX/UI || Graphic Design || 3D Character

GMTK 2days jam entry Created from the prompt "built to scale" our team came up with a reference to the popular Getting Over It and Goat Simulator, where the players take the role of a robotic goat and have to get used to their unique slit-eyed vision and movement.
